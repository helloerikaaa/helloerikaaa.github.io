I'm an award-winning software engineer and master's student in Data Science with 3+ years of experience in data analysis and 10+ years of experience in software development.

I hold a Bachelor’s Degree with honors in Computer Engineering with a focus on computer vision. My thesis project is titled “Mobile application for automatic counting and preliminary identification of bacterial colonies in culture dish using pattern recognition”. The purpose of this project is to develop a standard counting method of bacterial colonies for non-technical and experienced people in the microbiological area, and a preliminary identification to reduce costs of reagents in future experimental tests.

Most of the teams I've been working with are multicultural and multinational. This not only has led me to deploy multiple and different software projects in other countries, but also I discovered a passion for languages and culture, which gives me a more diverse point of view of how to be more proactive and hummble at the moment of developing software.

I'm Chief Data Officer of a startup called TrackChain, where we aim to reinvent the logistics business using Artificial Intelligence techniques.

My personal interests are:
- Data Science
- Application of machine learning in real-world situations
- Scientific Programming
- DevOps
- Fullstack development

I hold a Bachelor’s Degree with honors in Computer Engineering with a focus on computer vision. My thesis project is titled “Mobile application for automatic counting and preliminary identification of bacterial colonies in culture dish using pattern recognition”. The purpose of this project is to develop a standard counting method of bacterial colonies for non-technical and experienced people in the microbiological area, and a preliminary identification to reduce costs of reagents in future experimental tests.

I have a strong background in full stack and mobile development, as work and academic experience.

At present, I'm working as Research engineer at Barcelona Supercomputing Center, lead data scientist in a startup named TrackChain and I'm freshman at MSc in Data Science.

My personal interests are:
 - Data Science
 - Application of deep learning in real-world situations
 - Fullstack development
 - DevOps
